## ⚠️ Visa Note for Netherlands

The Netherlands uses the **Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant)** permit — not EU Blue Card.
- Employer must be a **recognised IND sponsor** and file on your behalf
- Processing time: ~2 weeks once submitted
- Salary threshold (2026): **€5,942/month gross** (age 30+) or **€4,357/month** (under 30)
- **30% tax ruling** applies for first 5 years — significant financial benefit
- No Dutch language required for most tech roles

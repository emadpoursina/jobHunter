# Country: Netherlands (NL)

- **Priority rank:** 3
- **Target role:** Full-stack / backend JavaScript developer
- **Migration route via job offer:** Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant) — employer must be IND-recognised sponsor
- **Language requirement:** English sufficient for tech (9/10 sampled offers); Dutch rarely required
- **Sponsorship common?:** Yes — 6/10 sampled offers explicitly offer visa/relocation
- **Known blockers:** Salary threshold €5,942/mo gross (30+) or €4,357/mo (under 30); employer-led process
- **Primary job boards / sources:**
  - LinkedIn, Indeed.nl, Nationalevacaturebank.nl
  - Relocate.me, Jaabz, Startup.jobs, nextleveljobs.eu
- **Notes:** ~2 week processing once submitted. **30% tax ruling** for first 5 years — major financial benefit. Amsterdam, Utrecht are primary hubs. Adyen, Booking.com, Miro routinely hire internationally.

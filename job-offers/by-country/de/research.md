## Visa Sponsorship Summary

Out of 10 offers collected:

- **5 explicitly offer visa sponsorship or relocation** (LemonOne, CHECK24, Zero to One Search, N26, VISARIGHT, NavVis)
- **4 did not mention visa sponsorship** (Bundesdruckerei, bracketlab, codeblick, unnamed SaaS)
- **0 explicitly excluded visa sponsorship**

> ℹ️ Important note from market research: In Germany, *visa sponsorship* typically means the employer issues an employment contract that enables you to apply for an EU Blue Card yourself — it is not the same as the US H-1B system where the company files the petition. Many companies that don't list "visa sponsorship" may still support the process once an offer is made.

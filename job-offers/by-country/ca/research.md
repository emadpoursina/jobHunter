## ⚠️ Visa Note for Canada (2026)

Canada uses the **Global Talent Stream (GTS)** as the primary fast-track work permit for tech roles:
- Processing time: **4–6 weeks** end-to-end in 2026 (target is 2 weeks, but real-world is longer)
- Employer must file a **Labour Market Benefits Plan (LMBP)** — no job ad requirement
- Full-Stack / Software Engineers are on the **ESDC in-demand occupations list** (Category B) — no referral needed
- GTS leads directly to **Express Entry PR** — one of the clearest pathways from work permit to permanent residency
- **Important:** Iranian nationals can obtain Canadian visitor/work visas. No current blanket restriction as of June 2026.
- Salary threshold: typically **$38.46+/hour** (~C$80K+/year) for tech roles in Toronto/Vancouver

## ⚠️ Visa Note for Portugal (2026)

Portugal uses two main visa routes for non-EU tech workers:

- **Tech Visa (Visto Tech):** A certification for companies — employers certified under this programme can sponsor non-EU workers through a simplified D3 process. Active certified sponsors include Farfetch, Google, Cloudflare, OutSystems, and Feedzai.
- **D3 Highly Qualified Activity Visa:** The individual worker visa. Salary threshold: 1.5x the national average (~€2,100–2,500/month gross minimum). Processing time: 1–2 months via the Portuguese consulate.
- **Important:** The **NHR tax regime** (flat 20% tax for new arrivals) ended for new applicants from 2024. Standard progressive Portuguese tax rates now apply.
- Iranian nationals: No current blanket restriction for Portugal as of June 2026. D3/Tech Visa pathway is open.
- Path to PR: 5 years of legal residence → permanent residency or citizenship.
